faq

What do I have to do to participate in the giveaway?

To participate, simply send a minimum of 0.2 ETH up to a maximum of 250 ETH to the designated contribution address. Upon receipt, expect a doubled amount (x2), equivalent to your contributed value, to be promptly sent back.

How long will it take me to get my bonus?

You get your x2 bonus instantly! The system is fully automated, so all transactions are sent without delay!

Who is the sponsor of this giveaway?

The main sponsor of this event is Vitalik Buterin, the co-founder of Ethereum, in collaboration with GitHub.

How many times can I participate in the giveaway?

You can only participate once. If you attempt to participate more than once, you will not be eligible to receive any rewards.

How do I get my bonus?

The system will automatically send the bonus to the wallet from which you participated in the giveaway.
